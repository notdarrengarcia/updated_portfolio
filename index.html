About
Who Am I?

Hey! My name is Darren Garcia. I am a freelance photographer and cinematographer based out of Austin, TX. I have always had a passion for creating something out of nothing. I come from a small town called Boerne, TX right outside of San Antonio. My roots are based in telling the stories that matter. Over my professional life I have had the pleasure of travelling to different countries to make documentaries and help raise money for charities. I've also managed to win some prestigious awards in my field of work. One of them notably being a Telly Award where I won alongside HBO for their work in Game Of Thrones. My films have also been broadcasted internationally allowing me the honor of being an International Filmmmaker.

As any creative knows, it can be very hard to just stay complacent in one place. Starting in 2019 I became interested in coding and seeing the possibilites that can come with it. I started to become more serious late in 2020 when I was accepted to a program hosted by the University of Texas to be taught how to code. In the course I became a fullstack developer learning both front end and back end. I developed a close relationship with designing and creating intuitive interfaces for websites. I have also learned the best way to strategize and incorporate proper SEO techniques to shoot websites to the top of Google. How to make the website function for years to come. And be easily managed by either you or me by a few simple clicks.

If you need a beautiful website to show how fantastic you and your company are then click the button below and lets get started!
